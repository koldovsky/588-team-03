About Colora Podcast

Colora Podcast is a completely independent and award-winning podcast
company. We aim at helping listeners be aware of the things they
don’t think about, better understand each other and the whole world.
Colora Podcast was founded in 2010 and is located in the heart of Brooklyn,
New York. Our podcasts are downloaded more than ten million times per
month by listeners from nearly 190 countries across the world. All of our
shows are powered by you, our listener.

Andrew Shimmer is the founder and producer of Colora Podcast, and also
the author of various supernatural stories. He has a passion for everything
historical, mystic, or extraordinary.

At present, Andrew also writes and produces his podcasts to Lore, Earwolf,
and Cabinet of Curiosities, and in addition works on the Amazon TV show,
performing on tour to promote his fresh books, and his podcasts for live
theater audiences. When he is not at work, he enjoys reading mystery
stories by the fireplace with a glass of fine red wine.
